Recognizing the importance of understanding the inner workings of websites for successful penetration testing, I've also embarked on a journey to master the MERN stack for web development. This commitment allows me to acquire comprehensive knowledge of modern web development techniques commonly utilized by professionals in the field.

In addition to my cybersecurity activities, I am actively engaged in the fascinating fields of Robotics and AI, constantly expanding my expertise in these cutting-edge technologies.

As a hobbyist, I spend time in game development using Unity, creating exciting and immersive digital experiences. Furthermore, I use Blender3D to explore the world of product design, occasionally taking on freelance projects to showcase my creative and technical skill.

Through my multifaceted journey in technology, I aim to contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity while also leaving my mark in the domains of web development, robotics, AI, game development, and product design. My GitHub repositories serve as a testament to my ongoing exploration and mastery of these diverse fields.
